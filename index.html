Ly Dinh, Ph.D

4202 E Fowler Avenue, CIS 2011 · Tampa, FL 33620-7800 · dinhly@usf.edu

I am a computational social science researcher studying how research methods such as network analysis, social simulation models, and text analysis, can be used to advance our understanding of various social and organizational systems. Her current projects place network science at the core to understand and explain a number of social and organizational phenomena ranging from egocentric networks to interagency emergency response networks.
